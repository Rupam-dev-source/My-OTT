Our Films and TV-shows


Films:Films (or movies) are typically longer productions, ranging from around 90 minutes to several hours. They are usually released in theaters, on streaming platforms, or on physical media like DVDs and Blu-rays. Films can belong to various genres, such as drama, comedy, action, horror, and science fiction, and they often tell a complete story within their runtime.


TV-shows:TV shows (or television series) are episodic productions that are broadcast on television or available on streaming platforms. They consist of multiple episodes, which can be part of a single season or span several seasons. TV shows can also belong to various genres and often develop characters and storylines over a longer period, allowing for more in-depth exploration of themes and narratives.
